The automations below run daily without any input from you. Connect to Make.com or Zapier to activate WhatsApp triggers.

Morning Reminder — 7:00am
WhatsApp message sent to each staff member with their personal checklist link. Triggered daily, 7 days a week.
Chase Message — 11:00am
If a staff member has not submitted by 11am, an automatic follow-up is sent. No submission = flagged in admin view.
Month-End Payroll Summary — 28th
On the 28th of each month, a summary PDF is generated showing each person's average completion % and their service charge multiplier for that month.
Photo Archive — Google Drive
All submitted photos are automatically filed to a Google Drive folder, organised by staff member and date.

To deploy: Host this file on Vercel or Netlify (free). Connect a Google Sheet as the database via Make.com. WhatsApp messages via Twilio or WATI. Total monthly cost: ~$0–$15 USD.
