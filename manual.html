1What the DCC is

Acronym: DCC = Daily Command Centre. One screen that shows everything you need to know about today. Think of it like the dashboard in a car — not the engine, just the gauges.
📍
Where it lives
pog-staff-app-2.vercel.app — on your phone, your laptop, your watch toolbar
🕒
When it refreshes
Every hour from 7am to 10pm Melbourne. Quiet overnight.
💬
How you act on it
Tap a →ollo button. That copies a ready instruction. You paste it into Cowork. ollo does the work.

2Morning — start of day

Five small things. Two minutes total. Done.
☕

Open the DCC

On your phone or laptop, open pog-staff-app-2.vercel.app. It's pinned to your iPhone home screen and your Mac toolbar.

If it looks like yesterday, tap the ↻ Refresh button (top-right).

👀

Read the SNAPSHOT line at the top

The amber strip tells you how fresh the page is. If it says "5 min ago" you're good. If it says "STALE" or you see red, tap Update DCC now in the purple Ask ollo bar.

🚨

Look at the red Approvals box

Each line is something waiting on a YES or NO from you. Tap the small → ollo button next to a line to ask ollo to act on it.

📝

Glance at TODAY

The purple "Today" cell shows what happened yesterday and what is still open. Quick scan, no need to read every word.

✅

Pick ONE urgent thing

Look at the red "Urgent" cell. Pick the top one. Tap the →ollo button. Switch to Cowork. Paste. Let ollo do the rest.

ADHD trick: never start with the whole list. Pick one. Win it. Then pick the next.

3During the day

The DCC is a snapshot. Cowork is the live driver. The buttons connect them.

The Ask ollo bar (purple, top of page)

Seven quick buttons. Tap one and a ready prompt goes to your clipboard. Examples:

ButtonWhat ollo will do when you paste it
Show all draftsLists every email sitting in PG Drafts so you can decide what to send.
Scan PG inbox 24hSkims the last 24 hours of mailmanchina@gmail.com and flags anything from key people.
Update DCC nowRebuilds the DCC with the latest state and pushes it to Vercel.

Check PG financeShows latest P&L, debit card balance, Tuesday payment run status.
ollo heartbeatAsks ollo to tell you what it's working on right now and what is stuck.

Per-line →ollo buttons

Every actionable line in Approvals, Today, and Urgent has a small →ollo button. Tap it and a context-specific prompt copies to clipboard. The line for "Send the 2 Erandi drafts" copies a prompt that already names the drafts. You don't have to type anything.

The free-text Ask ollo box

If none of the buttons fit, type your own instruction in the white-ish box and hit Enter or Copy. The page auto-prefixes "ollo:" so ollo knows it's a command.

⚠ One step is still manual

The DCC can't talk to Cowork directly — that's an Apple security limit, not a bug. So the flow is always: 1) Tap button → 2) Switch to Cowork app → 3) Paste with Cmd+V → 4) Press Enter. Four steps. Five seconds.

4Night — close the day

Tells ollo the day is done so tomorrow's gen starts in the right place.
  • Glance at the "Do it" Apple Note — clear anything that's now done
  • Send any drafts you decided on (FROM = ollo)
  • Approve / reject the Tuesday payment run if it's still open
  • Tap ollo out (close day) in the Ask ollo bar — paste — Enter

"ollo out" writes a 1-page summary of the day to your Daily Continuity Drive folder. Tomorrow's 7am gen reads that file first.

6Why it sometimes shows old info

The DCC is a snapshot, not a live mirror.

The page is built and pushed every hour 7am–10pm Melbourne. Between pushes nothing on the page changes — even if you sent a draft 2 minutes ago. That's why the Approvals box can show "2 drafts to send" when Drafts is actually empty.

The amber bar at the top tells you the age. Under 60 minutes is normal. Over 90 minutes the bar goes red — that means a gen failed somewhere and you should tap Update DCC now.

For truly live state — use Cowork

If you want to know what's actually in Drafts right now, ask ollo. Tap "Show all drafts" in the Ask ollo bar. That reads Gmail live. The DCC web page never will.

7If something looks wrong

Three things that have actually happened, and what to do.
What you seeWhat to do
The page still shows yesterday's day Tap ↻ Refresh top-right. If still wrong, tap Update DCC now in the Ask ollo bar.
The amber bar is red — "SNAPSHOT STALE" A gen failed. Open Cowork and paste: ollo: check ~/ollo/dcc-push-errors.log and tell me what failed
Approvals shows things you already did That's a stale snapshot, not a bug. Tap Update DCC now and wait 60 sec for the new push.
A →ollo button copied nothing iPhone Safari sometimes blocks clipboard. Long-press the button instead and pick "Copy". If still nothing, take a screenshot — tell ollo and we'll fix it.
Cowork doesn't recognise what you pasted Make sure the prompt starts with the word ollo:. The buttons add it automatically; the free-text box adds it too.
