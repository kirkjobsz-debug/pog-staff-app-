💬 Ask ollo — tap a button, then paste into Cowork
How to use this board (3 steps):
1. Tap a button below. It copies a ready message.
2. Open the Cowork app (where you talk to ollo).
3. Paste (Cmd or long-press) and press Enter. That is it.
Every →ollo button anywhere on this page works the same way. Buttons never send anything on their own. Nothing here changes your files or emails until you paste it into Cowork and ollo does it.
What each button does (plain words)
📧 Show all drafts — lists every email sitting in Drafts, waiting for you to send.
🔍 Scan PG inbox 24h — checks the last day of email and flags anything from the team, SRP, finance or guests.
🔄 Update DCC now — rebuilds THIS board with the latest drafts, calendar and finance, then republishes it.
➕ Add idea — drops a note into your "Do it" list so it is not lost. Type your idea where it says.
💰 Check PG finance — the money picture right now: bank balance, the Tuesday payment run, anything outstanding.
❤️ ollo heartbeat — asks ollo what it is working on and what is blocking it.
🌙 ollo out (close day) — tells ollo you are done; it saves the day's notes and updates the handover file.
